Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

VeritasBid is a contractor tool built and operated by Site Veritas. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use VeritasBid, how we use it, where it is stored, and what rights you have over your data.

1. Information We Collect

When you unlock the Premium tier of the VeritasBid calculator, we collect:

  • First name
  • Business name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Calculator inputs — job type, square footage, labor, equipment, materials, variable costs, overhead, and margin settings you entered during your session
  • Calculated bid prices — floor, target, and premium bid figures generated by the tool
  • Browser user agent — for analytics and debugging purposes

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive financial account data.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Unlock the full bid intelligence report within the calculator
  • Follow up with relevant resources, updates, or offers related to VeritasBid and Site Veritas services
  • Improve the accuracy and usefulness of the bid calculator over time
  • Provide aggregate analytics on how contractors use the tool (non-identifying)

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Data Storage & Third-Party Services

Your information is stored and processed using the following services:

  • GoHighLevel (GHL) — Your contact details (name, business, email, phone) are sent to GoHighLevel, our CRM platform, where they are stored as a contact record tagged with "VeritasBid". GoHighLevel's privacy policy applies to data stored on their platform.
  • Netlify — The VeritasBid application and its serverless functions are hosted on Netlify. Session data may pass through Netlify's infrastructure.
  • localStorage (your browser) — To save you from re-entering your information on return visits, your unlock details are stored locally in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device unless you submit the form again.

4. Cookies & Local Storage

VeritasBid uses browser localStorage (not cookies) to remember that you have already unlocked the Premium tier. This data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to any server. You can clear it at any time by clicking "Switch contractor" in the app or clearing your browser's site data.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your contact record from our systems

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@siteveritas.com with your name and email address. We will respond within 30 days.

6. Data Retention

Contact records are retained in GoHighLevel for as long as you remain an active contact or until you request deletion. Calculator session data logged to our dashboard is retained for up to 24 months for analytics purposes.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of VeritasBid after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
